About Vale Instruments


Vale Instruments has been crafting violins for nearly 25 years. Known around the world for superb craftsmanship, Vale Instruments only selecting and utilizing the finest of acoustic tone woods.

Each Vale violin is meticulously crafted and inspected to ensure that each instrument delivers a consistent signature sound. Vale believes that the wood selection directly impacts the violin’s individual tonal signature, so Vale match up the highest quality tone wood to craft all of our violin to create a stunning violin that is both pleasing to the eye as well as the ear.

Our combination of craftsmanship, technique, and a skilled team of artisans allows Vale to build the best bowed and other instruments at an affordable price.
